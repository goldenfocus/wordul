ARISE

verb · 2 syllables · /ə'raɪz/

ARISE

What does "ARISE" mean?

To come into being, occur, or move upward into view.

Meanings

  1. To come into existence or notice; to occur or emerge. Should any problems arise, call the front desk.
  2. To get up or rise to a standing position. He bade the kneeling knight arise. formal
  3. To result or originate from a cause. Most of the confusion arose from a single mistyped figure.

Word origin

From Old English 'arisan', combining the intensive prefix 'a-' with 'risan' ('to rise'); cognate with German 'erstehen'.

Remember it

ARISE = A + RISE; just put 'A' in front of RISE and the meaning rises right out of it.
