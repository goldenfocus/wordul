GAYER

adjective · 2 syllables · /'ɡeɪ.ər/

GAYER

What does "GAYER" mean?

The comparative form of gay: more cheerful, brighter, or more homosexual.

Meanings

  1. More homosexual, or more openly identified with LGBTQ culture. The neighborhood got gayer and prouder after the first Pride march.
  2. More merry, lively, or brightly colored (now somewhat archaic in this sense). The streets grew gayer as the festival lanterns were lit. archaic

Word origin

Comparative of 'gay', from Old French 'gai' (joyful, lively), of uncertain Germanic origin; the 'homosexual' sense became dominant in English through the 20th century.

Remember it

GAYER = GAY + ER; the '-er' is the same comparative tail in 'happier' and 'brighter'.
