GOLLY

What does "GOLLY" mean?

A mild exclamation of surprise, wonder, or emphasis.

Meanings

  1. An expression of surprise, astonishment, or mild enthusiasm. Golly, I didn't expect to see you here! informal

Word origin

A 19th-century euphemism for 'God', altering the word to avoid taking the holy name in vain - the same minced-oath impulse behind 'gosh' and 'gee'.

Remember it

GOLLY softens 'God' into a giggle - swap the harsh 'd' for two friendly L's and you can say it in front of grandma.
