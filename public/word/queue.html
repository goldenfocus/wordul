QUEUE

noun · 1 syllable · /kjuː/

QUEUE

What does "QUEUE" mean?

A line of people or things waiting their turn for something.

Meanings

  1. A line of people or vehicles waiting for something. There was a long queue outside the bakery.
  2. A data structure or list in which items are processed in the order they arrive. The print job was added to the back of the queue. technical
  3. To form or join a line; to line up. Fans queued overnight for the release.

Did you know?

  • 'Queue' is pronounced exactly like the letter Q - the four letters after it add no new sound - and it means 'tail', from Latin 'cauda', because a line of people trails like one.

Word origin

From French 'queue' meaning 'tail', from Latin 'cauda' (tail); a line of waiting people resembles a long tail trailing behind.

Remember it

QUEUE is a Q followed by four silent letters waiting in line behind it - the word is its own queue.
