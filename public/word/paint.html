PAINT

noun · 1 syllable · /peɪnt/

PAINT

What does "PAINT" mean?

A colored liquid spread over a surface to protect or decorate it, or the act of applying it.

Meanings

  1. A liquid coating of pigment that dries to color and protect a surface. A fresh coat of paint made the old door look new.
  2. To apply paint to a surface. They spent the weekend painting the fence white.
  3. To create a picture or image with paint. She paints landscapes in oils.
  4. To describe something vividly in words. The report painted a grim picture of the economy. figurative

Word origin

From Old French 'peint', the past participle of 'peindre' (to paint), from Latin 'pingere', to paint or embroider.
