KITTY

noun · 2 syllables · /'kɪt.i/

KITTY

What does "KITTY" mean?

An affectionate or childish name for a cat or kitten.

Meanings

  1. A pet name for a cat, especially a young one. The kitty curled up on the windowsill in the sun. informal
  2. A pool of money collected from several people for shared use or as a stake. Everyone threw a few dollars into the kitty for the office party. informal
  3. In card games, the pile of chips or cards set aside as a pot or reserve. The winning hand took the whole kitty.

Word origin

As 'cat', from a pet form of 'kit' (short for 'kitten'); the gambling sense may come from 'kit' as a collection of pooled stakes, or be a separate development.
