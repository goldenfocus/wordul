PHOTO

noun · 2 syllables · /'foʊ.toʊ/

PHOTO

What does "PHOTO" mean?

A picture made by capturing light on a sensor or film; a photograph.

Meanings

  1. A photograph; an image recorded by a camera. She framed a photo of the whole family on the beach.
  2. To photograph someone or something. The magazine photoed the actor for its cover. informal

Did you know?

  • 'Photograph' literally means 'drawing with light' from the Greek - and the oldest surviving one, Niepce's view from a window made around 1826, took hours of sunlight to expose.

Word origin

A clipping of 'photograph', from Greek 'phos/photos' (light) plus 'graphein' (to draw or write); the word means, literally, 'drawing with light'.

Remember it

PHOTO carries the Greek 'photos' for light - every photo is light caught and kept.
