LEFTY

noun · 2 syllables · /'lɛf.ti/

LEFTY

What does "LEFTY" mean?

An informal term for a left-handed person.

Meanings

  1. A person who is left-handed. As a lefty, she always smudged her ink dragging her hand across the page. informal
  2. A person with left-wing political views. The columnist dismissed the proposal as a lefty fantasy. informal
  3. In baseball, a left-handed pitcher or batter; a southpaw. They brought in a lefty from the bullpen to face the slugger. informal

Word origin

From 'left' (Old English 'lyft', meaning weak or foolish) plus the diminutive suffix '-y', an American coinage of the late 19th century.

Remember it

LEFTY ends in -Y like the wave of a left hand waving goodbye to the right.
