TENTH

adjective · 1 syllable · /tenθ/

TENTH

What does "TENTH" mean?

Coming after ninth in order, or one of ten equal parts of a whole.

Meanings

  1. Constituting number ten in a sequence; the ordinal form of ten. This is her tenth marathon this year.
  2. One of ten equal parts; a fraction equal to one divided by ten. A dime is a tenth of a dollar.
  3. A musical interval spanning an octave plus a third. His left hand could stretch a tenth on the piano. technical

Word origin

From Old English 'tēoþa', the ordinal of 'tīen' (ten), later reshaped to match 'ten'; the 'th' ending marks the ordinal as in 'fourth' and 'fifth'.

Remember it

TENTH = TEN + TH: just stack the ordinal ending 'th' onto 'ten', the same move that makes 'fourth' and 'sixth'.
