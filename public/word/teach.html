TEACH

verb · 1 syllable · /tiːtʃ/

TEACH

What does "TEACH" mean?

To help someone learn a subject or skill by giving instruction or guidance.

Meanings

  1. To impart knowledge or skill to someone through instruction. She taught me to read music before I could spell my own name.
  2. To cause someone to learn a lesson through experience or consequence. The cold winter taught them to store more wood. figurative
  3. To work as a teacher; to give lessons as a profession. He teaches at the local high school.

Word origin

From Old English 'tæcan' meaning to show, point out, or instruct, related to 'token' and Old English 'tācn' (a sign); the underlying sense is to make something known by showing a sign.
