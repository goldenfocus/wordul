APTLY

adverb · 2 syllables · /'æpt.li/

APTLY

What does "APTLY" mean?

In a way that is appropriate or fitting to the situation.

Meanings

  1. In a manner suited to the circumstances; fittingly. The storm-battered ship was aptly named Resilience.

Word origin

From 'apt', via Old French 'apte' from Latin 'aptus', the past participle of 'apere' ('to fasten, attach'), plus the adverb-forming suffix '-ly'.

Remember it

APTLY = APT + LY: when something is APT, add LY and it fits the sentence just as APTly as it fits the world.
