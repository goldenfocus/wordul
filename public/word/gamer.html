GAMER

noun · 2 syllables · /'ɡeɪ.mər/

GAMER

What does "GAMER" mean?

A person who plays video games or tabletop games, especially regularly and enthusiastically.

Meanings

  1. Someone who plays video games, often as a serious hobby. As a lifelong gamer, she'd finished the series three times over.
  2. A player of tabletop or role-playing games. The gamers met every Friday for another session of their long campaign.
  3. A person who plays gambling games. The casino floor was crowded with late-night gamers. formal

Did you know?

  • 'Gamer' is far older than the console: it appears in English centuries before video games, originally meaning a gambler or a player of games of chance.

Word origin

From 'game' (Old English 'gamen', amusement, joy) plus the agent suffix '-er'; the video-game sense became dominant in the late 20th century.

Remember it

GAMER = GAME + R. Add the 'R' (the player) to the GAME and you get the one holding the controller.
