GUIDE

noun · 1 syllable · /ɡaɪd/

GUIDE

What does "GUIDE" mean?

A person or thing that shows the way, leads, or advises.

Meanings

  1. A person who leads or directs others, especially on a journey. Our guide knew every trail on the mountain.
  2. A book or document offering information or instruction. She bought a guide to the city's hidden cafes.
  3. To lead, direct, or influence the course of someone or something. He guided the boat carefully between the rocks.
  4. A device or marking that directs motion along a path. A metal guide kept the saw blade running straight. technical

Word origin

From Old French 'guider', altered under Italian influence from an earlier 'guier', from a Frankish word related to 'wit' and 'witan' meaning to know or to watch.
