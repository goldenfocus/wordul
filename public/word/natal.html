NATAL

adjective · 2 syllables · /'neɪ.təl/

NATAL

What does "NATAL" mean?

Relating to the place or time of one's birth.

Meanings

  1. Of or relating to birth. Doctors monitor a baby's health from the natal period onward.
  2. In astrology, of the chart cast for the exact moment of a person's birth. She had her natal chart read on her thirtieth birthday. technical

Did you know?

  • Vasco da Gama named the South African region Natal because he first sighted its coast on Christmas Day, 1497 - 'natal' for the birth, the Nativity, of Christ.

Word origin

From Latin 'natalis', 'of birth', from 'natus', past participle of 'nasci', 'to be born'.

Remember it

NATAL = born + 'AL'; it shares its first three letters with 'native' and 'nation', all from 'being born'.
