TREND

noun · 1 syllable · /trɛnd/

TREND

What does "TREND" mean?

A general direction in which something is developing or changing.

Meanings

  1. A prevailing tendency or direction of change over time. The data shows a clear upward trend in global temperatures.
  2. A current fashion or popular style. Baggy jeans came back as a trend just as everyone had thrown theirs out.
  3. A topic gaining sudden popularity on social media. The dance became a trend overnight and then vanished by Friday. informal
  4. To move or develop in a general direction; to become popular online. Her offhand tweet started trending within the hour.

Word origin

From Old English 'trendan', to turn or roll; the modern sense of a general drift or tendency emerged in the 18th-19th centuries.

Remember it

A TREND tells you which way things BEND - both share the 'end' sound and the idea of a turning curve.
