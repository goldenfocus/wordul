PATIO

noun · 3 syllables · /ˈpæ.ti.oʊ/

PATIO

What does "PATIO" mean?

A paved outdoor area beside a house, used for sitting and dining.

Meanings

  1. A paved or surfaced area adjoining a house, typically used for relaxing or eating outdoors. They grilled dinner on the patio as the sun went down.
  2. An inner courtyard open to the sky, in Spanish-style architecture. A fountain trickled in the shaded patio at the heart of the house.

Word origin

From Spanish 'patio' (inner courtyard), of uncertain origin, possibly from Old Occitan 'patu' (pasture, untilled land) via Latin 'pactum' (an enclosed agreement) or from Latin 'patere' (to lie open).
