FIFTY

noun · 2 syllables · /'fɪf.ti/

FIFTY

What does "FIFTY" mean?

The number equal to five times ten; 50.

Meanings

  1. The cardinal number 50. Half of one hundred is fifty.
  2. Amounting to fifty in number. There were fifty chairs set out for the lecture.
  3. A banknote or sum worth fifty units of currency. She paid with a crisp fifty and waved off the change. informal

Did you know?

  • The United States has only had fifty states since 1959 - Alaska joined in January and Hawaii in August of that single year, locking in the number on the flag.

Word origin

From Old English 'fiftig', combining 'fif' (five) with '-tig' (a group of ten), of Germanic origin.

Remember it

FIFTY = FIVE tens; the '-ty' ending means 'times ten', so sixty, seventy, eighty all share the trick.

What it teaches

Fifty is just halfway to a hundred and a whole life past zero; it depends which end you face.
