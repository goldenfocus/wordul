What does "WOMAN" mean?

An adult human female.

Meanings

  1. An adult female human being. The woman beside me on the train was reading poetry.
  2. Women considered collectively; the female sex. A debate about the place of woman in ancient society. formal

Did you know?

  • 'Woman' literally means 'female person': it began as Old English 'wifman', where 'wif' meant female and 'man' simply meant a human of any sex.

Word origin

From Old English 'wifman', a compound of 'wif' ('woman, female') and 'man' ('human being, person'); the 'f' assimilated over time and the first vowel rounded, giving the modern 'woman'. 'Man' originally meant any human, not specifically male.

Remember it

WOMAN contains 'wo' + 'man' - but historically the 'wo' is 'wif' (female) and 'man' just meant 'person'.
