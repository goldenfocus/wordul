HOBBY

noun · 2 syllables · /'hɒb.i/

HOBBY

What does "HOBBY" mean?

An activity done regularly for pleasure in one's leisure time rather than for pay.

Meanings

  1. A pursuit or activity engaged in for relaxation and enjoyment outside of work. Birdwatching started as a hobby and quietly took over his weekends.
  2. A small Old World falcon (genus Falco) known for swift, agile flight. A hobby hunted dragonflies above the reeds at dusk. technical

Did you know?

  • Your weekend pastime is named after a toy: 'hobby' is short for 'hobbyhorse', the child's stick-horse, with 'Hobby' an old nickname for the name Robert.

Word origin

Shortened from 'hobbyhorse', a toy or wickerwork horse; 'hobby' was originally a pet name for a small horse, from the medieval given name 'Robin/Robert' applied to ponies.

Remember it

HOBBY has two B's like two busy hands - the things you do with both hands free.
