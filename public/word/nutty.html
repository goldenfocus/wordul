NUTTY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'nʌt.i/

NUTTY

What does "NUTTY" mean?

Tasting of or containing nuts; also, informally, eccentric or crazy.

Meanings

  1. Having a flavour or texture reminiscent of nuts. The aged cheese had a rich, nutty finish.
  2. Crazy, foolish, or eccentric. It sounds nutty, but his wild plan actually worked. informal
  3. Enthusiastically fond of something or someone. She's absolutely nutty about old jazz records. informal

Word origin

From 'nut' plus '-y'; the 'crazy' sense grew from older slang in which 'nut' meant the head, so to be 'off one's nut' was to be out of one's mind.

Remember it

NUTTY doubles its 'T' like a squirrel doubling its stash - and a nutty person has gone a bit 'off their nut'.
