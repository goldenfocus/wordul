CHUMP

noun · 1 syllable · /tʃʌmp/

CHUMP

What does "CHUMP" mean?

A foolish or gullible person who is easily tricked.

Meanings

  1. A person who is easily fooled or taken advantage of; a sucker. Only a chump would pay full price the day before the sale. informal
  2. A short, thick lump of wood; a blunt block. He split the chump of oak for the winter fire. archaic

Word origin

Originally a thick lump of wood, perhaps a blend of 'chunk' and 'lump' or 'stump'; the 'foolish person' sense grew from comparing a dull head to a block of wood.
