WIDEN

verb · 2 syllables · /'waɪ.dən/

WIDEN

What does "WIDEN" mean?

To make or become wider; to increase in breadth or extent.

Meanings

  1. To make something broader from side to side. The city plans to widen the highway to ease traffic.
  2. To become broader or larger in extent or scope. Her eyes widened in surprise at the news.
  3. To increase the range or reach of something abstract. Travel widens your understanding of other cultures. figurative

Word origin

Formed in English from the adjective 'wide' (Old English 'wid', meaning 'spacious, broad') plus the verb-forming suffix '-en', which turns an adjective into 'to make or become'.

Remember it

WIDE + N: add an N to 'wide' and the road itself gets longer and broader.
