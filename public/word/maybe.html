MAYBE

adverb · 2 syllables · /ˈmeɪbi/

MAYBE

What does "MAYBE" mean?

Possibly but not certainly; perhaps.

Meanings

  1. Used to express possibility or uncertainty; perhaps. Maybe it will rain, so bring an umbrella.
  2. Something that is uncertain or undecided; a possibility. I need a yes or no, not another maybe. informal

Word origin

A contraction of the Middle English phrase 'it may be', meaning 'it is possible that'.

Remember it

MAYBE is literally 'may be' shrunk into one word - the possibility ('may') is still hiding at the front.
