FIZZY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'fɪ.zi/

FIZZY

What does "FIZZY" mean?

Producing many small bubbles; effervescent.

Meanings

  1. Bubbly and effervescent, releasing carbon-dioxide bubbles with a hissing sound. She prefers fizzy water to flat.
  2. Lively, exuberant, or buzzing with energy. There was a fizzy excitement in the room before the show. figurative

Word origin

From 'fizz', an imitative word echoing the hissing sound of escaping gas, plus the adjective suffix '-y'; recorded from the early 19th century.

Remember it
