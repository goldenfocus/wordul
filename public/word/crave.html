CRAVE

verb · 1 syllable · /kreɪv/

CRAVE

What does "CRAVE" mean?

To feel a powerful, often physical, desire for something.

Meanings

  1. To want something intensely, especially food or comfort. Pregnant, she suddenly craved pickles at midnight.
  2. To ask for or beg something earnestly. I crave your indulgence for one more moment. archaic

Word origin

From Old English 'crafian', meaning 'to demand or claim as a right'; the sense softened over centuries from formal demand to intense longing.

Remember it
