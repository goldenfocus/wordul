ABOUT

preposition · 2 syllables · /ə'baʊt/

ABOUT

What does "ABOUT" mean?

On the subject of; concerning, or approximately.

Meanings

  1. Concerning; on the subject of. The book is about a boy and his dog.
  2. Approximately; near to a number or amount. It costs about twenty dollars.
  3. In the surrounding area; here and there. There were toys scattered about.
  4. On the verge of (about to). She was about to leave when the phone rang.

Word origin

From Old English 'onbutan', a compound of 'on' (on) and 'butan' (outside, around) - originally meaning 'on the outside of' or 'around'.

Remember it

ABOUT hides 'A BOUT' - a bout is a round, and 'about' once meant 'around'.
