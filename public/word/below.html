BELOW

preposition · 2 syllables · /bɪ'loʊ/

BELOW

What does "BELOW" mean?

At or to a lower position than something; underneath.

Meanings

  1. At a lower level or position than something else. The valley lay far below the ridge.
  2. Less than a stated amount, rank, or standard. The temperature dropped below freezing overnight.
  3. At or to a lower place; further down a page or text. See the footnote below for the source.

Word origin

From Middle English 'bilooghe', a compound of 'bi' ('by') and 'logh' ('low'), formed on the model of older 'alow'.

Remember it

BELOW = 'be low'. Whatever is below simply chooses to be low.
