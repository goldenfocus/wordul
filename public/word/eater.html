EATER

noun · 2 syllables · /'iː.tər/

EATER

What does "EATER" mean?

A person or animal that eats, especially in a specified way.

Meanings

  1. One who eats, often described by manner or preference. He's a picky eater who quarantines the peas on his plate.
  2. A variety of fruit, especially an apple, suited for eating raw rather than cooking. This apple is a crisp eater, too sweet for a pie. informal

Word origin

From Old English 'etere', the agent noun of 'etan' (to eat) formed with the '-er' suffix that marks the doer of an action.

Remember it

EATER = EAT + ER: the doer suffix turns the verb into the one who does it.
