PRANK

noun · 1 syllable · /præŋk/

PRANK

What does "PRANK" mean?

A trick played on someone, usually as a joke, to amuse or mildly embarrass them.

Meanings

  1. A mischievous trick or practical joke. Their classic prank was filling his office with balloons.
  2. To play a trick on someone. She pranked her brother with a fake lottery ticket. informal

Word origin

Of uncertain origin, recorded from the 16th century; possibly related to the obsolete verb 'prank' meaning to dress or adorn showily, perhaps from a Germanic root.
