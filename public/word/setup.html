SETUP

noun · 2 syllables · /'sɛt.ʌp/

SETUP

What does "SETUP" mean?

The way something is arranged, organized, or configured.

Meanings

  1. The arrangement or configuration of equipment, a system, or an organization. Their home-office setup had three monitors and a standing desk.
  2. The opening part of a joke or story that prepares for the punchline. The setup was long, but the punchline landed perfectly.
  3. A scheme to deceive, frame, or trap someone. He swore the whole arrest was a setup. informal
  4. The process of installing or preparing software or a device for use. The printer setup took only a few minutes. technical

Word origin

A compound of the verb 'set' plus 'up', written as a single noun; 'set' descends from Old English 'settan' (to cause to sit, place).

Remember it

SETUP is just 'set up' fused into a noun - you SET things UP, then live with the setup.
