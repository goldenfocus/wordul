What does "SPORT" mean?

An activity involving physical exertion and skill, often competitive and governed by rules.

Meanings

  1. A physical, usually competitive, game or pastime such as football, tennis, or swimming. Cricket is the most popular sport in much of South Asia.
  2. A person who behaves fairly and gracefully, especially in defeat. Be a good sport and let your little brother win once. informal
  3. To wear or display proudly. He arrived sporting a bright orange tie and a grin. informal
  4. An organism showing a sudden spontaneous genetic mutation differing from the parent. The seedless variety began as a chance sport on one branch. technical

Did you know?

  • The word 'sport' originally meant pleasant diversion of any kind, not athletics: it is a clipped form of 'disport', from Old French for 'to carry oneself away' from work and worry.

Word origin

A shortened form of Middle English 'disport', meaning diversion or amusement, from Old French 'desporter', literally 'to carry away' (from cares).
