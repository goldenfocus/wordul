SPEND

verb · 1 syllable · /spɛnd/

SPEND

What does "SPEND" mean?

To pay out money for goods or services.

Meanings

  1. To pay out money in exchange for something. They spend most of their paycheck on rent.
  2. To use up or pass time in a particular way. We spent the afternoon untangling fishing line.
  3. To use up or exhaust a resource or one's energy. By the last mile the runners had spent every reserve.
  4. An amount of money paid out, especially in marketing or business. The agency doubled its ad spend for the holidays. informal

Word origin

From Old English 'spendan', borrowed from Latin 'expendere' (to pay out, weigh out), from 'ex-' (out) plus 'pendere' (to weigh).

Remember it

SPEND ends the same as 'end' - what your money does when you spend it.
