GAWKY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'ɡɔː.ki/

GAWKY

What does "GAWKY" mean?

Awkwardly tall, ungainly, or clumsy, especially in an adolescent way.

Meanings

  1. Ungainly and awkward in movement or posture, often from being lanky. He was a gawky teenager, all elbows and knees that hadn't caught up to him.

Word origin

From dialectal English 'gawk' (to stare stupidly, or an awkward person), perhaps related to 'gaw' (to gaze) from Old Norse 'ga' (to heed).

Remember it

GAWKY starts like GAWK - the gawky one is the one who keeps gawking instead of watching where the limbs go.
