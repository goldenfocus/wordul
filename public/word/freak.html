FREAK

noun · 1 syllable · /frik/

FREAK

What does "FREAK" mean?

A person, animal, or event markedly unusual or abnormal.

Meanings

  1. An abnormal or strikingly unusual person, thing, or occurrence. The hailstorm in July was a real freak of weather.
  2. A person obsessively devoted to a particular interest. She's a control freak about how the dishwasher is loaded. informal
  3. Highly unusual and unexpected. A freak accident left the experienced climber stranded.
  4. To react or cause to react with sudden extreme emotion. Don't freak out - the noise was just the cat. informal

Word origin

Of uncertain origin, first recorded in the 16th century meaning a sudden whim or caprice; possibly from an Old English root 'frician' to dance or move briskly.
