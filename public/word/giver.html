GIVER

noun · 2 syllables · /'ɡɪv.ər/

GIVER

What does "GIVER" mean?

A person who gives, especially one who is generous with time, help, or gifts.

Meanings

  1. One who gives; a person inclined to share, donate, or provide. She was a natural giver, always the first to offer help.

Word origin

From 'give' plus agentive '-er', from Old English 'giefan' (to give), of Germanic origin.

Remember it

GIVER = GIVE + R; the receiver takes, the giver hands over - same suffix, opposite roles.

A little poem

She kept the smaller half and called it fair,
handed the warm bread, the better chair-
and somehow ended every day more full.

tercet

Wordplay

  • I tried to out-generous my friend, but the giver always has the upper hand - literally, it's the one doing the handing.

What it teaches

The giver rarely runs empty; what you hand to others has a strange way of filling the space it left.
