What does "PUBIC" mean?

Relating to the lower front region of the pelvis or the area around the genitals.

Meanings

  1. Of or relating to the region of the lower abdomen near the genitals, or the pubic bones of the pelvis. The X-ray showed a hairline fracture in the pubic bone. technical

Did you know?

  • The pubic symphysis - the cartilage joint linking the two pubic bones - can soften and stretch during late pregnancy, loosened by the hormone relaxin to ease childbirth.

Word origin

From Latin 'pubes' ('the groin, adult, signs of maturity'), the same root behind 'puberty' and 'pubescent'.

Remember it

PUBIC and PUBERTY share the Latin 'pubes': both mark the body's coming of age.
