ALBUM

noun · 2 syllables · /'æl.bəm/

ALBUM

What does "ALBUM" mean?

A bound collection of photos, stamps, or other items, or a single body of recorded music.

Meanings

  1. A blank book for mounting photographs, stamps, or keepsakes. She kept the wedding photos in a leather album.
  2. A collection of recordings released as a single work. The band's third album went platinum.

Did you know?

  • An 'album' is literally a 'white thing': it comes from Latin 'albus' (white), the same root as 'albino' and 'albumen', the white of an egg.

Word origin

From Latin 'album', a white tablet on which public notices were written, from 'albus' (white); the blank white page later became any blank book for collecting things.

Remember it
