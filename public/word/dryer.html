DRYER

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈdraɪər/

DRYER

What does "DRYER" mean?

A machine or device that removes moisture, especially one for drying laundry or hair.

Meanings

  1. An appliance that dries laundry by tumbling it in heated air. She pulled the warm towels straight from the dryer.
  2. A device for drying something, such as hair or hands. The hair dryer hummed as she got ready for work.
  3. Comparative of 'dry'; more dry (often spelled 'drier'). The desert air felt even dryer than yesterday.

Word origin

An agent noun from the verb 'dry' (Old English 'drygan', to make dry) plus the suffix '-er' meaning 'a thing that does'.

Remember it

DRYER = 'DRY' + 'ER': the thing (-er) that makes your clothes dry.
