PLACE

noun · 1 syllable · /pleɪs/

PLACE

What does "PLACE" mean?

A particular position, point, or area in space.

Meanings

  1. A specific location or spot. This is the place where we first met.
  2. A position in a sequence, order, or ranking. She finished in third place.
  3. A person's role, station, or proper position in society. He never forgot his place at the table.
  4. To put something in a particular position. Place the cup on the saucer.
  5. To identify or recognise someone from memory. I know her face but I can't place her. informal

Word origin

From Latin 'platea', an open space or broad street, itself from Greek 'plateia' (broad way); via Old French 'place' into English.

Remember it

PLACE shares its root with 'plaza' and 'piazza' - all from the Latin for a broad open square.
