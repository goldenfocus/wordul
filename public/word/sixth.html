SIXTH

adjective · 1 syllable · /sɪksθ/

SIXTH

What does "SIXTH" mean?

Coming after fifth in order or position; constituting one of six equal parts.

Meanings

  1. Next after the fifth in a sequence or ranking. She finished sixth in a field of forty runners.
  2. One of six equal parts of a whole. Each heir received a sixth of the estate.
  3. A musical interval spanning six diatonic degrees of the scale. The melody leaps up a major sixth on the second line. technical

Word origin

From Old English 'siexta', from the Proto-Germanic ordinal of 'six' (Old English 'siex'), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European '*sweks'.

Remember it

SIXTH ends in -XTH, one of English's rarest letter clusters - say 'siks-th' and you can feel why kids stumble on it.
