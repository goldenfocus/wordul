noun · 3 syllables · /ˈɔːdiˌoʊ/

AUDIO

What does "AUDIO" mean?

Sound, especially when recorded, transmitted, or reproduced electronically.

Meanings

  1. Recorded or transmitted sound, or the sound portion of a recording or broadcast. The video looked sharp but the audio kept cutting out.
  2. Relating to the recording, transmission, or reproduction of sound. He upgraded to a proper audio interface for his podcast.

Did you know?

  • The 'audio' range humans can hear spans about 20 to 20,000 Hz - and the top end erodes with age, so most adults can no longer hear the highest tones a child easily catches.

Word origin

From Latin 'audio', 'I hear', from 'audire' ('to hear'); adopted in English in the early 20th century as a prefix and standalone word for sound technology.

Remember it

AUDIO starts the same as 'audible' and 'audience' - all from Latin 'audire', to hear.
