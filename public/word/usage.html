USAGE

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈjuː.sɪdʒ/

USAGE

What does "USAGE" mean?

The way in which something is used, or the amount of it that is used.

Meanings

  1. The manner in which words and language are actually used by speakers. The dictionary records modern usage rather than dictating it.
  2. The amount of something consumed or used over a period. Their water usage spiked during the summer heatwave.
  3. An established custom or habitual practice. By long usage, the youngest child opens the first gift. formal

Word origin

From Old French 'usage' ('custom, practice'), from Latin 'usus' ('a using'), the past participle of 'uti' ('to use').

Remember it

USAGE = USE + AGE: how a word's USE shifts with the AGE it lives in.
