EIGHT

noun · 1 syllable · /eɪt/

EIGHT

What does "EIGHT" mean?

The cardinal number equal to seven plus one, written 8.

Meanings

  1. The number 8, one more than seven. Three twos and a two make eight.
  2. Amounting to eight in number. There were eight chairs around the long table.
  3. A rowing crew or boat of eight oarsmen. The college eight pulled away in the final stretch. technical

Word origin

From Old English 'eahta', from a Proto-Indo-European root '*okto' shared by Latin 'octo' and Greek 'okto'; the silent 'gh' is a fossil of an old guttural sound.

Remember it

Turn the numeral 8 on its side and it becomes the lemniscate, the symbol for infinity.
