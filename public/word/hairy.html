HAIRY

adjective · 2 syllables · /ˈheə.ri/

HAIRY

What does "HAIRY" mean?

Covered with hair or having a lot of hair.

Meanings

  1. Having much hair, or covered with hair-like growth. He rolled up his sleeve to show a thick, hairy forearm.
  2. Frighteningly difficult, dangerous, or full of risk. Landing the small plane in that crosswind got pretty hairy. informal

Word origin

From Old English 'hǣr' (hair), of Germanic origin, plus the adjectival suffix '-y'.

Remember it

HAIRY is just HAIR plus Y - the extra letter sprouts off the end like one more strand.
