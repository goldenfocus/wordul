PIECE

noun · 1 syllable · /piːs/

PIECE

What does "PIECE" mean?

A part or portion of something, or a single created or distinct item.

Meanings

  1. A part or portion separated from a whole. Cut me a piece of bread.
  2. A single instance or specimen of something, especially a work of art, writing, or music. She performed a difficult piece by Chopin.
  3. An object used in a board game, such as a chess or checkers token. He moved his last piece across the board.
  4. To assemble or repair by joining parts, usually with 'together'. Detectives pieced together the events of that night.

Word origin

From Old French 'piece' (a bit, fragment, coin), from Vulgar Latin 'pettia', probably of Gaulish Celtic origin meaning a part or thing.

Remember it

PIECE follows the rule 'I before E' - and a piece of pie has both the spelling and the snack.
