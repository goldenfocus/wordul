COMFY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'kʌm.fi/

COMFY

What does "COMFY" mean?

Comfortable; giving a pleasant feeling of ease and physical relaxation.

Meanings

  1. Comfortable and cozy; providing physical ease. She curled up in the comfy old armchair with a book. informal
  2. Feeling relaxed and untroubled in a situation. I'm not comfy making that decision without him. informal

Word origin

An informal shortening of 'comfortable,' from Old French 'conforter' (to strengthen, console), from Latin 'confortare' (to strengthen greatly).

Remember it

COMFY is COMFORTable with the middle squeezed out - just like sinking into a chair, the long word relaxes into a short one.
