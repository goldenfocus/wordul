SPOON

What does "SPOON" mean?

An eating or cooking utensil with a small shallow bowl on a handle.

Meanings

  1. A utensil consisting of a shallow bowl at the end of a handle, used for eating, stirring, or serving. He stirred two sugars into the coffee with a long spoon.
  2. The quantity a spoon holds; a spoonful. Add a spoon of honey to soothe the tea. informal
  3. To lie close together front-to-back, mirroring each other's curves. They spooned under the blanket while the rain drummed the roof. informal

Did you know?

  • The word 'spoon' began as Old English 'spon', a chip or splinter of wood: the earliest spoons were carved wooden slivers, and only later did the name narrow to the eating utensil.
