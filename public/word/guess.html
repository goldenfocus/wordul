GUESS

verb · 1 syllable · /ɡɛs/

GUESS

What does "GUESS" mean?

To form an opinion or estimate without enough information to be sure.

Meanings

  1. To estimate or suppose something without certain knowledge. Can you guess how many marbles are in the jar?
  2. To suppose or believe; to think. I guess we should leave before the traffic builds. informal
  3. An estimate or conjecture. My best guess is somewhere around noon.

Word origin

From Middle English 'gessen', probably of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse 'geta' meaning to get or to guess, and to the modern 'get'.
