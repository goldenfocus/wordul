ERROR

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈɛr.ər/

ERROR

What does "ERROR" mean?

A mistake or inaccuracy; something done wrong or in violation of correctness.

Meanings

  1. A mistake; an act, judgment, or statement that is incorrect. A single typing error changed the meaning entirely.
  2. A condition in which a machine or program fails to behave correctly. The app crashed with a fatal error. technical
  3. A misplay charged against a fielder in baseball that lets a runner advance. The shortstop's error let the winning run score. technical

Did you know?

  • The most famous error on the internet, '404 Not Found', is just an HTTP status code - 4xx means the client asked for something that does not exist, and 04 is the specific stray.

Word origin

From Latin 'error' (a wandering, a straying), from 'errare' (to wander, to go astray), the same root behind 'err' and 'erratic' - a mistake is, at root, a wandering off course.

Remember it
