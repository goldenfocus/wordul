GEEKY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'ɡiː.ki/

GEEKY

What does "GEEKY" mean?

Characteristic of an obsessive enthusiast, especially one devoted to niche or technical interests.

Meanings

  1. Devoted to or absorbed in an intellectual, technical, or niche pursuit, often socially unfashionably so. She has a geeky obsession with vintage synthesizers. informal

Did you know?

  • The root of 'geeky' is a grim one: a 'geek' was originally a carnival freak-show performer who bit the heads off live chickens for a paying crowd.

Word origin

From 'geek' plus '-y'; 'geek' derives from dialectal 'geck' (a fool), from Low German, and once named a carnival performer who bit heads off live animals.

Remember it

GEEKY has a double 'E' like the wide eyes behind a pair of thick glasses.
