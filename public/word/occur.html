OCCUR

verb · 2 syllables · /ə'kɜr/

OCCUR

What does "OCCUR" mean?

To happen or take place, especially without being planned.

Meanings

  1. To happen or come to pass. The accident occurred just minutes after the warning light came on.
  2. To be found or exist in a particular place. These rare orchids occur only on a handful of limestone hills.
  3. To come into one's mind, often suddenly. It never occurred to her that he might be lying.

Word origin

From Latin 'occurrere', 'to run to meet, run against', from 'ob-' (toward) plus 'currere' (to run); a thing that 'occurs' originally ran up to meet you.

Remember it

OCCUR runs at you with a double C, just as its Latin root 'currere' means 'to run'.
