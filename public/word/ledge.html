LEDGE

What does "LEDGE" mean?

A narrow horizontal surface projecting from a wall, cliff, or other vertical face.

Meanings

  1. A flat shelf-like projection on a building or rock face. A pigeon nested on the window ledge.
  2. A shelf of rock, especially one underwater or on a cliff. The climbers rested on a narrow ledge halfway up.
  3. A continuous ridge or seam of rock containing ore. The miners followed a quartz ledge deep into the hill. technical

Word origin

From Middle English 'legge', probably from 'leggen' meaning to lay; a ledge is, in effect, something laid out as a shelf.
