DIGIT

noun · 2 syllables · /'dɪdʒ.ɪt/

DIGIT

What does "DIGIT" mean?

Any of the numerals from 0 to 9; also, a finger or toe.

Meanings

  1. Any one of the ten basic numerals, 0 through 9. Her phone number has the same digit four times in a row.
  2. A finger, thumb, or toe. The frostbite cost the climber two digits on his left hand. technical

Did you know?

  • We have ten numerical digits for the same reason a word for 'finger' became a word for 'number': humans first counted on the ten digits of their hands.

Word origin

From Latin 'digitus', meaning finger or toe; numerals are called digits because people first counted on their fingers.

Remember it

DIGIT means both finger and number - because we learned numbers by counting on our fingers.

Wordplay
