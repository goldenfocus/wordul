REUSE

verb · 2 syllables · /ri'juːz/

REUSE

What does "REUSE" mean?

To use something again, especially instead of discarding it.

Meanings

  1. To use an object or material more than once rather than throwing it away. We reuse the glass jars to store spices.
  2. The act or practice of using something again. The campaign promotes reuse over single-use plastics.

Word origin

Formed in English from the prefix 're-' ('again', from Latin) and 'use', which came via Old French 'user' from Latin 'usus', past participle of 'uti', to use.

Remember it

The verb 'reuse' (ri-YOOZ) ends in a buzzing Z; the noun 'reuse' (ri-YOOS) ends in a soft S - the same spelling, two sounds.
