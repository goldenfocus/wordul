noun · 2 syllables · /ˈædˌmɪn/

ADMIN

What does "ADMIN" mean?

Short for administration or administrator; the routine work or person that manages a system or organization.

Meanings

  1. The routine clerical and organizational tasks of running something. Half my morning vanished into email and admin. informal
  2. A person with privileged control over a computer system, network, or online community. Message an admin if you get locked out of the server. informal
  3. Relating to administration, as in an admin account or admin panel. You'll need admin rights to install that. informal

Word origin

A 20th-century clipping of 'administration' and 'administrator', from Latin 'administrare' (to manage, serve), formed from 'ad-' (to) plus 'ministrare' (to serve).
