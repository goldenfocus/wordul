PENNY

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈpɛn.i/

PENNY

What does "PENNY" mean?

A coin of small value; the smallest unit of currency in the US and UK.

Meanings

  1. A coin worth one cent in the US or one-hundredth of a pound in the UK. He found a penny shining on the sidewalk.
  2. A very small amount of money. The painting didn't cost them a penny. informal

Word origin

From Old English 'penig' or 'pening', a Germanic coin name of uncertain origin, related to Dutch 'penning' and German 'Pfennig'.
