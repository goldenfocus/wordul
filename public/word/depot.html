DEPOT

noun · 2 syllables · /'diː.poʊ/

DEPOT

What does "DEPOT" mean?

A place where goods, vehicles, or supplies are stored or where transport services are based.

Meanings

  1. A storehouse or warehouse for goods, supplies, or military equipment. The relief workers stacked blankets and water at the supply depot.
  2. A railway or bus station, especially a small one (chiefly North American). We waited at the old train depot until the last car pulled in.
  3. A place where buses, trains, or trams are kept and maintained. Every night the empty buses return to the depot for cleaning. technical

Word origin

From French 'dépôt' meaning a deposit or store, from Latin 'depositum', a thing laid down or entrusted.

Remember it

DEPOT and 'deposit' share the same Latin root - both mean a place where things are laid down.
