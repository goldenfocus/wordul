BRAND

noun · 1 syllable · /brænd/

BRAND

What does "BRAND" mean?

A name, logo, or identity that distinguishes one maker's goods from another's.

Meanings

  1. A type of product made by a particular company under a recognizable name. She only buys one brand of coffee.
  2. A mark burned onto livestock or goods to show ownership. Every steer on the ranch carries the same brand on its flank.
  3. To mark with a hot iron, or to label someone with a lasting characterization. The press branded him a traitor for the rest of his life.
  4. A piece of burning or charred wood. He pulled a glowing brand from the fire to light the torch. archaic

Did you know?

  • The word 'brand' started as the burning stick used to scorch ownership marks into cattle hide - the marketing meaning literally comes from fire.

Word origin

From Old English 'brand' meaning a burning piece of wood or a sword blade; the meaning shifted through the burned ownership mark on cattle to the modern commercial sense.
