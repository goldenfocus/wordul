MEDIA

noun · 3 syllables · /ˈmiːdiə/

MEDIA

What does "MEDIA" mean?

The main channels of mass communication, such as television, press, and the internet, considered collectively.

Meanings

  1. The collective means of mass communication: broadcasting, publishing, and online platforms. The story spread across every form of media in hours.
  2. The plural of 'medium': different materials or means of artistic or technical expression. She works in mixed media, combining paint and collage. technical
  3. Storage or transmission materials for data, such as disks or tapes. Back up the files onto removable media. technical

Word origin

Latin 'media', plural of 'medium' (middle, intermediate agency); the mass-communication sense is a 20th-century shortening of 'mass media'.

Remember it

MEDIA sits in the MIDdle - it's Latin for the middle thing that carries a message from sender to you.

Wordplay

What it teaches

Every medium is a middleman; the message you receive has already paid a toll in the shape of the channel.
