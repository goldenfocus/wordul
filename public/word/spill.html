SPILL

verb · 1 syllable · /spɪl/

SPILL

What does "SPILL" mean?

To cause or allow liquid to flow out of a container by accident.

Meanings

  1. To let liquid or loose material flow over the edge of its container unintentionally. He spilled coffee across the keyboard reaching for his phone.
  2. To reveal information one was meant to keep private. Promise you won't spill the surprise before Saturday. informal
  3. An instance of liquid being spilled, or the liquid itself. The oil spill stretched for miles along the coast.

Word origin

From Old English 'spillan' (to destroy, kill, waste); the meaning shifted toward 'shed or pour out liquid' by the late Middle Ages.

Remember it

SPILL has two 'l's standing side by side like the two streams a spill splits into.
