SEVEN

noun · 2 syllables · /'sɛv.ən/

SEVEN

What does "SEVEN" mean?

The number equal to one more than six; the cardinal number 7.

Meanings

  1. The cardinal number that follows six and precedes eight. Three plus four makes seven.
  2. Amounting to seven in number. There are seven days in a week.

Did you know?

  • Psychologist George Miller's famous 1956 paper argued that human short-term memory holds about seven items, give or take two - the 'magical number seven'.
  • Seven is the likeliest roll of two dice: of the 36 possible combinations, six of them add up to seven, more than any other total.

Word origin

From Old English 'seofon', from Proto-Germanic 'sebun', from Proto-Indo-European 'septm', the same root as Latin 'septem' and Greek 'hepta'.
