AFIRE

adjective · 2 syllables · /əˈfaɪər/

AFIRE

What does "AFIRE" mean?

On fire, or burning with strong feeling.

Meanings

  1. Burning; on fire. By dawn the barn was afire and the sky orange with it. literary
  2. Filled with intense passion, excitement, or eagerness. The whole crowd was afire with the news. figurative

Word origin

Formed in Middle English from the prefix 'a-' (meaning 'on' or 'in', from Old English 'on') plus 'fire' - the same 'a-' pattern seen in 'aglow', 'aflame', 'asleep', and 'ablaze'.

Remember it

AFIRE is just 'a fire' with the gap closed - say it aloud and you hear the flames you mean.
