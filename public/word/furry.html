FURRY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'fɜːr.i/

FURRY

What does "FURRY" mean?

Covered with fur or resembling fur in softness and texture.

Meanings

  1. Covered with or made of fur. A furry kitten curled up on the warm windowsill.
  2. Soft and fuzzy to the touch, like fur. The peach had a furry skin she didn't like to bite through.
  3. Coated with a soft fuzzy layer, often unpleasantly, as on a neglected surface. After the flu he woke with a furry tongue and a pounding head. informal

Word origin

From 'fur' (from Old French 'forrer', to line or sheathe, from a Germanic root meaning a sheath or covering) plus the adjective suffix '-y'.

Remember it

FURRY doubles the R - two R's for the two layers of a thick winter coat.
