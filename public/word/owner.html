OWNER

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈoʊ.nər/

OWNER

What does "OWNER" mean?

A person who owns or possesses something.

Meanings

  1. A person or entity that legally possesses or holds title to something. The owner of the dog was fined for letting it roam.

Word origin

From the verb 'own', from Old English 'agan' meaning 'to possess', plus the agent suffix '-er'; the same Old English root that gives 'owe' and 'ought'.

Remember it

OWNER = one who can say 'I OWN' - the verb 'own' with '-er' for the person.
