TASTY

adjective · 2 syllables · /ˈteɪsti/

TASTY

What does "TASTY" mean?

Having a pleasant, appetizing flavor; delicious.

Meanings

  1. Having a good or appealing flavor. That was a surprisingly tasty veggie burger.
  2. Attractive, appealing, or impressive (of something other than food). The band laid down a tasty guitar riff. informal

Word origin

Formed in English from the noun 'taste' (via Old French 'taster', to sample) plus the adjective-forming suffix '-y' meaning 'full of, having the quality of'.

Remember it

TASTY = TASTE without the 'e', plus 'y' - the flavor that makes you say yes.
