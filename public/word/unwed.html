UNWED

adjective · 2 syllables · /ʌnˈwɛd/

UNWED

What does "UNWED" mean?

Not married.

Meanings

  1. Not married; single. The census tracked the rising number of unwed adults living alone.

Word origin

Formed in English from the prefix 'un-' ('not') plus 'wed', from Old English 'weddian' ('to pledge, marry'), related to 'wedd' ('a pledge').

Remember it

UN-WED: literally 'not wed' — the ring never went on.

A little poem

No vow, no veil, no borrowed name to wear-

What it teaches
