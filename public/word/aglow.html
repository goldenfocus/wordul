AGLOW

adjective · 2 syllables · /əˈɡloʊ/

AGLOW

What does "AGLOW" mean?

Glowing with warmth, light, or feeling.

Meanings

  1. Shining with a soft, warm light. The windows were aglow as we came up the lane. literary
  2. Radiant with a strong, happy emotion. Her face was aglow with pride. figurative

Word origin

Formed from the prefix 'a-' ('on, in', from Old English 'on') plus 'glow' - the same productive pattern that gives 'afire', 'aflame', 'ablaze', and 'asleep'.

Remember it

AGLOW = 'a glow' with the gap closed; the word itself seems lit from inside, like the thing it names.
