MONTH

What does "MONTH" mean?

One of the twelve named divisions of the year, roughly the time between new moons.

Meanings

  1. Each of the twelve periods, ranging from 28 to 31 days, into which the calendar year is divided. Her birthday falls in the coldest month of the year.
  2. A period of about 30 days, or the interval between the same dates of consecutive months. The lease runs for six months from today.

Did you know?

  • 'Month' and 'moon' are the same word in disguise: in Old English a 'monath' was literally one cycle of the 'mona', the moon, before fixed calendars cut the tie.

Word origin

From Old English 'monath', directly related to 'mona', the moon - a month was originally one cycle of the moon's phases.

Remember it

MONTH starts with MO, just like MOON - the month was once one full cycle of moonlight.
