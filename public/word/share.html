SHARE

verb · 1 syllable · /ʃɛər/

SHARE

What does "SHARE" mean?

To divide and distribute something with others, or a portion belonging to one person.

Meanings

  1. To give a portion of something to others, or to use something jointly. The children learned to share their toys.
  2. To tell others about your thoughts, feelings, or information. She shared the news with her closest friends.
  3. A part or portion belonging to or contributed by an individual. Everyone paid their fair share of the bill.
  4. One of the equal units of ownership into which a company is divided. He bought a hundred shares of the company. technical

Word origin

From Old English 'scearu', meaning a division or cutting, related to 'shear' and to 'plowshare'; the financial sense developed from the idea of a divided portion.
