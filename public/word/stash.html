STASH

verb · 1 syllable · /stæʃ/

STASH

What does "STASH" mean?

To store or hide something safely, especially in a secret place.

Meanings

  1. To put away or hide something for safekeeping or concealment. He stashed the cash under a loose floorboard. informal
  2. A secret store or collection of something. She kept a stash of chocolate in her desk drawer. informal

Word origin

An English coinage from around 1800, of uncertain origin, possibly a blend of 'stow' and 'cache'.

Remember it

STASH = STORE + CACHE smashed together: hide your secret stash in a cache you can store.
