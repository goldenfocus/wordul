CRATE

noun · 1 syllable · /kreɪt/

CRATE

What does "CRATE" mean?

A slatted wooden or plastic box used for storing or transporting goods.

Meanings

  1. A large container, often of wooden slats, for packing and moving goods. The movers loaded the crate of dishes into the truck.
  2. To pack something into a crate for storage or shipping. They crated the sculpture carefully before the flight.
  3. An old or dilapidated vehicle or aircraft. That rusty old crate barely made it up the hill. informal

Word origin

Probably from Latin 'cratis' meaning 'wickerwork, lattice, hurdle'; the same root gives English 'hurdle' and 'grate'.

Remember it
