PINKY

noun · 2 syllables · /'pɪŋ.ki/

PINKY

What does "PINKY" mean?

The smallest finger of the human hand; the little finger.

Meanings

  1. The little finger, the smallest of the five fingers. She hooked her pinky around his to seal the promise. informal
  2. Somewhat pink in color, a variant of 'pinkie' or 'pinkish'. The dawn sky had a pinky glow. informal

Did you know?

  • Your 'pinky' is a Dutch loanword: 'pink' meant 'little finger' in Dutch, and the name crossed the Atlantic with settlers of New Netherland, today's New York.

Word origin

From Dutch 'pinkje', diminutive of 'pink' (little finger); carried into American English by Dutch settlers, especially in the New York area.

Remember it
