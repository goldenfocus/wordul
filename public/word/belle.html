BELLE

noun · 1 syllable · /bɛl/

BELLE

What does "BELLE" mean?

A beautiful woman, especially the most admired one at a particular event or place.

Meanings

  1. A beautiful and charming woman, often the most admired at a gathering. She was the belle of every ball she attended that season.

Word origin

From French 'belle', the feminine of 'beau' ('beautiful'), from Latin 'bellus' meaning 'pretty, handsome, fine'.

Remember it

BELLE rhymes with 'bell' - the belle of the ball is the one every bell rings for.

A little poem

The whole room turned the way that flowers turn-
she never asked them to, and they still learn.

couplet

Wordplay

  • The town's most admired woman opened a phone company - now she's the belle who rings everyone.

What it teaches

To be admired by a room is easy; to leave it kinder than you found it is the rare beauty.
