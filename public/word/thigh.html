THIGH

noun · 1 syllable · /θaɪ/

THIGH

What does "THIGH" mean?

The part of the leg between the hip and the knee.

Meanings

  1. The upper part of the human leg, from hip to knee, containing the femur. She rested her hands on her thighs and caught her breath.
  2. The corresponding cut of meat on a bird or animal. He grilled the chicken thighs until the skin crisped.

Did you know?

  • Hidden in your thigh is the femur - the longest and strongest bone you own, able to bear loads many times your body weight.

Word origin

From Old English 'þēoh', from Proto-Germanic '*þeuhą', related to a root meaning 'to swell'; cognate with Dutch 'dij'.
