GODLY

adjective · 2 syllables · /ˈɡɒd.li/

GODLY

What does "GODLY" mean?

Devoutly religious; deeply pious or in keeping with the will of God.

Meanings

  1. Showing deep reverence for God; pious and morally upright. She was raised by godly parents who prayed each evening.
  2. Of or relating to God; divine in nature. The hymn spoke of a godly love beyond measure. formal

Word origin

From 'god' plus the suffix '-ly' meaning 'having the qualities of'; recorded in English from the 16th century.

Remember it

GODLY is simply GOD plus 'LY' - the suffix turns the noun into 'living in a God-like way'.
