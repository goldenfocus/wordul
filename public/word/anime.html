ANIME

noun · 3 syllables · /ˈæn.ɪ.meɪ/

ANIME

What does "ANIME" mean?

A style of animation originating in Japan, marked by vivid art and varied genres.

Meanings

  1. Japanese animated film and television, or the distinctive visual style associated with it. She stayed up all night finishing the new anime series.

Did you know?

  • In Japan the word 'anime' means any animation at all, including Disney films - the narrow 'Japanese-only' meaning exists only outside Japan.

Word origin

From Japanese 'anime', a clipped borrowing of the English word 'animation'; in Japan it refers to all animation, but in English it specifically means Japanese-style work.

Remember it

ANIME = ANImation with the M-E ending you say out loud: 'ann-ih-may'.
