PENNE

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈpɛn.eɪ/

PENNE

What does "PENNE" mean?

Short tube-shaped pasta cut diagonally at both ends.

Meanings

  1. A pasta in the form of short, ridged or smooth tubes with ends cut at a slant. She tossed the penne with tomato sauce and basil.

Did you know?

  • Penne means 'quills' in Italian: the pasta's slanted, pointed ends were thought to look like the sharpened nib of an old feather pen.

Word origin

From Italian 'penne', plural of 'penna' (feather, quill pen), so named because the diagonal-cut tube resembles the sharpened nib of a quill pen.

Remember it

PENNE = PEN-shaped pasta, cut at an angle like a nib; it even contains 'PEN'.
