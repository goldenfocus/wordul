ABODE

noun · 2 syllables · /ə'boʊd/

ABODE

What does "ABODE" mean?

The place where someone lives; a dwelling or home.

Meanings

  1. A house or home, often used formally or with mild grandeur. Welcome to my humble abode. formal
  2. In law, a person's settled, legally recognized place of residence. Police listed his abode as no fixed address. technical
  3. Past tense of 'abide': remained, dwelt, or waited. He abode in the village all his long life. archaic

Word origin

From Middle English 'abod', a noun formed from the verb 'abiden' ('to abide, remain'); literally 'a place where one abides'.
