ARTSY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'ɑːrt.si/

ARTSY

What does "ARTSY" mean?

Showily or self-consciously artistic, often to a pretentious degree.

Meanings

  1. Affectedly or ostentatiously concerned with art and aesthetics. The cafe was all exposed brick and artsy black-and-white prints. informal
  2. Having a genuine, appealing artistic flair (used approvingly). She has an artsy eye for arranging mismatched things beautifully. informal

Word origin

A 20th-century informal coinage from 'art' plus the diminutive-adjective suffix '-sy' (as in 'folksy' or 'cutesy'), often carrying a tinge of mockery.

Remember it

ARTSY = ART + SY; it's 'art' wearing a casual sweater - the '-sy' makes it sound a little too pleased with itself.
