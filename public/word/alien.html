ALIEN

noun · 3 syllables · /'eɪ.li.ən/

ALIEN

What does "ALIEN" mean?

A being from another world, or a person who is a foreigner or outsider.

Meanings

  1. A hypothetical or fictional being from another planet. The film imagines aliens who communicate only in music.
  2. A person who is not a citizen of the country where they live; a foreigner. The form asked whether he was a resident alien. formal
  3. Unfamiliar, foreign, or contrary to one's nature. Cruelty was alien to her gentle temperament.

Did you know?

  • 'Alien' meant a human foreigner for centuries before it meant a being from space; it shares the Latin root 'alius' (other) with 'alias' and 'alibi'.

Word origin

From Latin 'alienus' (belonging to another, foreign), from 'alius' (other); via Old French into English, long before any space-faring sense.

Remember it

ALIEN contains 'A LIE' - and an outsider is someone whose truth feels foreign to you.
