GAZER

noun · 2 syllables · /'ɡeɪ.zər/

GAZER

What does "GAZER" mean?

A person who gazes; one who looks long and steadily at something.

Meanings

  1. One who looks intently or fixedly, often in wonder or contemplation. Among the night's stargazers she was the quietest gazer of all.

Word origin

From 'gaze' plus agentive '-er'; 'gaze' is Middle English, of Scandinavian origin, akin to Norwegian dialect 'gasa' (to stare).

Remember it

GAZER hides 'gaze' plus an 'r' - the one who gazes, with eyes fixed wide.
