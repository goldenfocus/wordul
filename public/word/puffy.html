PUFFY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'pʌf.i/

PUFFY

What does "PUFFY" mean?

Swollen, soft, and rounded, as if filled with air.

Meanings

  1. Soft and rounded as though inflated; bulging out. Puffy white clouds drifted over the meadow.
  2. Swollen, especially of the face or eyes from crying, illness, or sleep. Her eyes were puffy after a night of weeping.

Word origin

From 'puff' (from Old English 'pyff', imitating the sound of blowing air) plus the adjective suffix '-y'.

Remember it

PUFFY doubles the F like a cloud doubling in size - blow a 'puff' and add a 'y'.
