BOOZE

noun · 1 syllable · /buːz/

BOOZE

What does "BOOZE" mean?

Alcoholic drink, especially when consumed in large amounts.

Meanings

  1. Alcoholic liquor. They stocked up on booze for the party. informal
  2. To drink alcohol, especially heavily. We boozed until well past midnight. informal

Word origin

From Middle English 'bousen' meaning 'to drink to excess', from Middle Dutch 'busen' ('to drink deeply').

Remember it

BOOZE ends in 'OOZE' - too much of it and your words start to ooze together.
