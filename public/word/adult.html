ADULT

noun · 2 syllables · /əˈdʌlt/

ADULT

What does "ADULT" mean?

A fully grown person or animal that has reached maturity.

Meanings

  1. A person who is fully grown and legally of age. The film is meant for adults, not children.
  2. Fully developed and mature; grown-up. She showed an almost adult calm for a twelve-year-old.
  3. Intended for or suitable only for grown-ups, often as a euphemism for explicit content. The site is restricted to adult material.
  4. To behave in a responsible, grown-up way, especially with reluctance. I've been adulting all day - taxes, dentist, the works. informal

Did you know?

  • 'Adult' and 'adolescent' are the same Latin verb caught at two moments: 'adolescere', to grow up - the adolescent is still mid-growth, the adult has finished it.

Word origin

From Latin 'adultus', past participle of 'adolescere' (to grow up) - which is also the root of 'adolescent', so the grown-up and the growing share one ancestor.
