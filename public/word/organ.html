ORGAN

What does "ORGAN" mean?

A part of the body with a specific function, or a large wind keyboard instrument.

Meanings

  1. A self-contained part of a living body that performs a particular function, such as the heart or lungs. The liver is the largest internal organ in the human body.
  2. A large keyboard instrument that produces sound by driving air through pipes. The cathedral organ shook the stone walls during the final chord.
  3. A newspaper, journal, or other medium that voices a group's views. The pamphlet was the official organ of the trade union. formal

Did you know?

  • The largest organ of the human body is not internal at all - it is the skin, which can cover roughly two square metres in an adult.

Word origin

From Greek 'organon', meaning tool or instrument, via Latin 'organum' and Old French 'organe'; the musical and bodily senses both descend from the idea of a working instrument.

Remember it
