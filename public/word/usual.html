USUAL

adjective · 3 syllables · /ˈjuː.ʒu.əl/

USUAL

What does "USUAL" mean?

Happening, done, or used most of the time; ordinary or customary.

Meanings

  1. Habitually or commonly occurring; customary. We met at the usual cafe on the corner.
  2. The thing a person normally has or orders. The bartender poured his usual without being asked. informal

Word origin

From Late Latin 'usualis' ('ordinary'), from Latin 'usus' ('use, custom'), arriving in English via Old French in the 14th century.
