ENJOY

verb · 2 syllables · /ɪn'dʒɔɪ/

ENJOY

What does "ENJOY" mean?

To take pleasure or delight in something.

Meanings

  1. To get pleasure or satisfaction from something. She enjoys long walks after dinner.
  2. To have the benefit or use of something good (often a right or advantage). The region enjoys a mild climate year-round. formal
  3. To have a good time (reflexive: enjoy oneself). Go on, enjoy yourself - you've earned it.

Word origin

From Old French 'enjoir' / 'enjoier', from 'en-' (in) plus 'joir' (to rejoice), from Latin 'gaudere' (to rejoice); the same root for joy gives 'joy' and 'rejoice'.

Remember it

ENJOY = 'en-' + 'JOY': to be IN joy is to enjoy.

What it teaches
