GAMMA

What does "GAMMA" mean?

The third letter of the Greek alphabet, used as a symbol in science, math, and finance.

Meanings

  1. The third letter of the Greek alphabet (Γ, γ). In the equation, gamma stood for the surface tension. technical
  2. A form of high-energy electromagnetic radiation, as in gamma rays. The detector picked up a sudden burst of gamma radiation from deep space. technical
  3. A measure of how an image's brightness responds to input, used in displays and photography. He adjusted the gamma until the shadows showed more detail. technical
  4. In finance, the rate at which an option's delta changes as the underlying price moves. The trader watched gamma closely as the stock approached the strike price. technical

Word origin

From Greek 'gamma', the name of the letter Γ, ultimately from the Phoenician letter 'gimel' (meaning camel); the Roman letters C and G both descend from it.

Remember it

GAMMA has the double-M of a 'mmm' - and it's the third letter, like the 'G' that starts the word 'Greek'.
