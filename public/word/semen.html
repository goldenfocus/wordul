What does "SEMEN" mean?

The fluid produced by the male reproductive system that carries sperm.

Word origin

Directly from Latin 'semen' meaning 'seed', from the root 'serere' (to sow), the same family that gives us 'seminal', 'disseminate', and 'seminary'.
