PUDGY

adjective · 2 syllables · /'pʌdʒ.i/

PUDGY

What does "PUDGY" mean?

Slightly fat in a soft, rounded, often endearing way.

Meanings

  1. Short and rather plump; chubby in a soft, rounded manner. The baby reached out with pudgy little fingers.

Word origin

From 'pudge' ('something short and thick'), a dialectal English word of uncertain origin, possibly related to 'podge' and 'pudsy'.

Remember it

PUDGY sounds like 'puddle' plus 'doughy' - soft, round, and squishable.
