adjective · 2 syllables · /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ/

MICRO

What does "MICRO" mean?

Extremely small; operating on a very small scale.

Meanings

  1. Very small or small-scale, especially in contrast to something larger. She invested in micro businesses across the region.
  2. A microcomputer, microprocessor, or microphone, shortened in casual speech. Plug the micro into the mixing board. informal
  3. A prefix meaning one millionth of a unit, or simply 'very small'. A micrometer is one millionth of a meter. technical

Did you know?

  • In the metric system, 'micro' precisely means one millionth - so a micron, or micrometer, is a thousandth of a millimeter, roughly the width of a strand of spider silk.

Word origin

From Greek 'mikros' (small); used as a combining form in scientific English and, since the 20th century, as a free-standing word.

Remember it

MICRO comes from Greek 'mikros', meaning small - the 'micro' in microscope, microbe, and microchip all just mean tiny.
