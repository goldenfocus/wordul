PIGGY

noun · 2 syllables · /'pɪg.i/

PIGGY

What does "PIGGY" mean?

A small or young pig, often used as a child's affectionate name for one.

Meanings

  1. A little pig; a piglet, especially in children's language. This little piggy went to market. informal
  2. A toe, in the children's nursery rhyme. She counted his toes: 'and this piggy stayed home.' informal
  3. Resembling a pig, especially in being greedy or having small eyes. He fixed the cake with piggy, hungry eyes. informal

Word origin

A diminutive of 'pig' with the affectionate suffix '-y'; 'pig' itself is of uncertain origin, recorded in Middle English as 'pigge', possibly from Old English.

Remember it

PIGGY is PIG with a curly extra 'GY' tail tacked on the end.

A little poem
