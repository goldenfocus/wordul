ADAPT

verb · 2 syllables · /ə'dæpt/

ADAPT

What does "ADAPT" mean?

To change or adjust to fit new conditions or a new purpose.

Meanings

  1. To make or become suited to new circumstances. Immigrants often adapt to a new culture within a generation.
  2. To alter a text or work for a different medium or audience. They plan to adapt the novel into a television series.
  3. In biology, for a species or organism to become better suited to its environment over time. Desert plants have adapted to survive on almost no water. technical

Word origin

From Latin 'adaptare' ('to fit to'), combining 'ad-' (to) and 'aptare' (to fit), from 'aptus' (fitted, apt) - the same root as 'apt' and 'aptitude'.

Remember it

ADAPT contains 'APT' - to adapt is to make something apt, a perfect fit for new conditions.
