EATEN

verb · 2 syllables · /'iː.tən/

EATEN

What does "EATEN" mean?

The past participle of eat: consumed as food.

Meanings

  1. Past participle of eat: having been consumed as food. By noon the cake had been eaten down to crumbs.
  2. Past participle of eat: gradually worn away or corroded. The hull was half eaten by rust below the waterline. figurative

Word origin

Past participle of 'eat', from Old English 'etan'; the strong-verb pattern eat / ate / eaten descends from a shared Germanic root with German 'essen'.

Remember it

EATEN = EAT + EN: add the EN ending and the meal is already gone.
