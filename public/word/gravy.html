noun · 2 syllables · /ˈɡreɪ.vi/

GRAVY

What does "GRAVY" mean?

A sauce made from the juices of cooked meat, usually thickened and served hot.

Meanings

  1. A savory sauce made from meat juices, often thickened with flour. He poured gravy over the mashed potatoes.
  2. Money or advantage obtained easily or unexpectedly; a bonus. We'd already covered costs, so the rest was gravy. informal
