SIXTY

noun · 2 syllables · /'sɪk.sti/

SIXTY

What does "SIXTY" mean?

The cardinal number equal to six times ten; 60.

Meanings

  1. The number 60. Sixty divided by four is fifteen.
  2. Amounting to sixty in number. The recipe needs sixty minutes in the oven.
  3. The years of a decade between 60 and 69, especially of a century or a person's age (the sixties). He took up painting in his sixties.

Did you know?

  • We split every hour into 60 minutes because the ancient Babylonians counted in base 60 - a system over 4,000 years old that still runs your clock.

Word origin

From Old English 'siextig', from 'siex' (six) plus '-tig', the suffix denoting groups of ten; cognate with German 'sechzig'.

Remember it

SIXTY = SIX + TY; the 'ty' is the same '-ty' (ten) in twenty, thirty and forty - it literally means 'six tens'.
