FIFTH

What does "FIFTH" mean?

Coming after fourth in order; the ordinal form of five.

Meanings

  1. Next in order after the fourth. She finished fifth in a field of forty runners.
  2. One of five equal parts of something. Each heir received a fifth of the estate.
  3. A musical interval spanning five notes of a scale. The opening of the anthem leaps up a perfect fifth. technical
  4. A bottle holding one fifth of a US gallon of liquor. He bought a fifth of bourbon for the celebration. informal

Did you know?

  • When Americans say they're 'taking the Fifth,' they mean the Fifth Amendment's right to stay silent rather than incriminate themselves - a protection ratified in 1791.

Word origin

From Old English 'fifta', the ordinal of 'fif' (five), of Germanic origin, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European 'penkwe' (five).

Remember it

FIFTH = FIVE turned into an order word; it keeps the 'fif-' of five and adds '-th' like fourth and sixth.
