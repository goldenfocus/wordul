MECCA

What does "MECCA" mean?

A place that attracts many people with a shared interest; from Mecca, Islam's holiest city.

Meanings

  1. A place regarded as a center that many people are drawn to, especially for a shared passion. Detroit was once a mecca for the auto industry. figurative
  2. The city in Saudi Arabia that is Islam's holiest site and the destination of the Hajj pilgrimage. Millions of pilgrims travel to Mecca each year.

Did you know?

  • Five times a day, Muslims around the world orient their prayers toward a single point: the direction of Mecca, known as the qibla, which aligns hundreds of millions of people on one invisible axis.

Word origin

From the proper name of the Saudi Arabian city Makkah, the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad; the generic 'place of pilgrimage' sense developed in English by figurative extension.
