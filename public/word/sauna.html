SAUNA

noun · 2 syllables · /'sɔː.nə/

SAUNA

What does "SAUNA" mean?

A small heated room used for dry or steam heat baths to induce sweating.

Meanings

  1. A wood-lined room heated to high temperatures, used for relaxation and sweating. After the icy lake, the sauna felt like stepping into a warm exhale.
  2. A session or period spent taking such a heat bath. They finished the day with a long sauna and a cold beer. informal

Did you know?

  • Finland has roughly 3.3 million saunas for about 5.5 million people - more than one for every two residents, making it one of the most sauna-dense nations on Earth.

Word origin

Borrowed directly from Finnish 'sauna', a word for the traditional heated bathhouse long central to Finnish culture.

Remember it

SAUNA keeps its Finnish spelling intact - 'AU' for the heat you 'ow' your way through.
