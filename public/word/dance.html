DANCE

noun · 1 syllable · /dæns/

DANCE

What does "DANCE" mean?

A series of rhythmic body movements, usually to music; or the act of performing them.

Meanings

  1. To move rhythmically to music, usually following a set sequence of steps. They danced until the band packed up at midnight.
  2. A particular sequence of steps and movements performed to music. She taught the children a simple folk dance.
  3. A social gathering at which people dance. He asked her to the school dance.
  4. To move lightly, quiveringly, or playfully. Sunlight danced on the surface of the pond. figurative

Word origin

From Old French 'dancier', of uncertain origin, possibly from a Frankish word meaning to draw or stretch; it largely replaced the native Old English words for dancing.
