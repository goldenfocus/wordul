TRUCK

noun · 1 syllable · /trʌk/

TRUCK

What does "TRUCK" mean?

A large road vehicle built for carrying heavy loads or goods.

Meanings

  1. A heavy motor vehicle designed to transport cargo. A delivery truck blocked the alley all morning.
  2. To transport goods by truck. They truck fresh produce north every night.
  3. Dealings or commerce; usually in the phrase 'have no truck with'. She would have no truck with rumours. formal

Word origin

From Old French 'troque' (barter) for the trade sense; the vehicle sense is likely a shortening of Latin-derived 'trochus' (iron hoop, wheel), from Greek 'trokhos', a wheel.
