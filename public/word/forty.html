FORTY

noun · 2 syllables · /ˈfɔːr.ti/

FORTY

What does "FORTY" mean?

The number equal to four times ten; 40.

Meanings

  1. The cardinal number 40. She blew out forty candles without a single one left lit.
  2. Amounting to forty in number. The recipe called for forty cloves of garlic.

Did you know?

  • Forty is the only number whose name has its letters in alphabetical order - F, O, R, T, Y - a quirk no other number word shares.

Word origin

From Old English 'feowertig', combining 'feower' (four) and '-tig' (group of ten); the modern spelling dropped the 'u' of 'four', unlike 'fourteen' and 'forty's' cousin 'fourth'.

Remember it

FORTY drops the U that FOUR has - remember: there is no 'u' in forty, even though four has one.
