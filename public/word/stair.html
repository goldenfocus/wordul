STAIR

noun · 1 syllable · /stɛər/

STAIR

What does "STAIR" mean?

One of a series of steps for moving between different levels of a building.

Meanings

  1. A single step in a flight of steps. She paused on the top stair to catch her breath.
  2. A set or flight of steps, usually plural as 'stairs'. He carried the boxes up the stairs two at a time.

Word origin

From Old English 'stæger', meaning a flight of steps, related to 'stigan', to climb or ascend, from Proto-Germanic 'staigri'.
