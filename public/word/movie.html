MOVIE

noun · 2 syllables · /'muː.vi/

MOVIE

What does "MOVIE" mean?

A story or sequence of events recorded as moving images and shown on a screen.

Meanings

  1. A motion picture; a recorded sequence of moving images, usually with sound, telling a story. They watched an old black-and-white movie with the lights off.
  2. A showing of such a film, or the cinema itself. We grabbed popcorn before the movie started. informal

Did you know?

  • 'Movie' was once a slangy, faintly disreputable word - it began around 1912 as a quick American clipping of 'moving picture', the way 'selfie' would later clip 'self-portrait'.

Word origin

A shortening of 'moving picture', first recorded in American English around 1909-1912 as a casual, originally slang term for the new medium.

Remember it

MOVIE = 'movi' from 'moving' + E; a movie is just 'moving' with the picture left to your imagination.
