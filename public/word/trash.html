TRASH

noun · 1 syllable · /træʃ/

TRASH

What does "TRASH" mean?

Discarded material or refuse; things thrown away as worthless.

Meanings

  1. Waste material, refuse, or rubbish that is thrown away. Take the trash out before the truck comes on Tuesday.
  2. Something of very poor quality. He called the sequel trash, but it still made a fortune. informal
  3. To wreck, vandalize, or destroy something. The band trashed the hotel room and got banned for life. informal
  4. To criticize or disparage harshly. Reviewers trashed the film weeks before anyone had seen it. informal

Word origin

Of uncertain origin, recorded from the 16th century meaning broken twigs or cuttings; possibly from a Scandinavian source (compare Norwegian dialect 'trask', rubbish).

Remember it

TRASH ends in ASH - what is left over once something has burned up its usefulness.
