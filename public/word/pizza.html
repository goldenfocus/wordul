PIZZA

What does "PIZZA" mean?

A flat round of baked dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients.

Meanings

  1. A dish of leavened flatbread baked with savoury toppings, originating in Italy. We split a margherita pizza straight from the wood-fired oven.

Did you know?

  • The classic Margherita is said to date to 1889, when a Naples pizzaiolo topped a pie in red, white, and green to honour Queen Margherita of Savoy and the new Italian flag.
  • Since 2010, true Neapolitan pizza has been an EU-protected Traditional Speciality Guaranteed - the dough, ingredients, and even the oven are defined by law.

Word origin

From Italian 'pizza', recorded in Gaeta in 997 CE; its earlier origin is debated, possibly from a Lombardic word related to 'bite' or from Greek/Latin roots for a flat bread.
